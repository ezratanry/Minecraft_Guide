Game Modes

No. Modes Description
1. Survival Players must collect resources, build structures while surviving in their generated world. You can interact with the local villagers, and at night defend against mobs that attack on sight.
2. Hardcore A survival mode variant that is locked to the hardest setting and has permadeath. Once dead, players would be banned from the world/server.
3. Creative Strips away the survival aspects of Minecraft and allows players to fly and easily create, destroy and have infinite access to all blocks. Mobs are also not affected by your presence.
4. Spectator Allows players to fly through blocks and watch gameplay without directly interacting. Players are invisible and do not have an inventory, but can teleport to other players.
